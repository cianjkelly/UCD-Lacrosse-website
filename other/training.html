Training is also open to anyone interested in getting involved in lacrosse. No experience is required – we have a range of skill levels on both teams; from never held a stick before to playing at a national level. We will also provide the equipment for you to try it out and see if you would like to keep it up! All you need is sports gear (and maybe a gumshield!).

Training is usually €20 a month due in advance, but if you just want to drop by to give lacrosse a whirl – you’re more than welcome free of charge (although contact us beforehand so we can ensure equipment is ready for you).

If you decide you like it and want to keep playing, payments can be made through Paypal.
